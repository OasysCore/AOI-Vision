## 时间线 & 执行

### 镜头 D1 — 数字人开场 (0s-5s)

**你在即梦的操作：**

```
1. 首页 → 数字人 → 选中你的数字人形象
2. 背景选"纯色深蓝"或上传一张科技感背景图
3. 声音选你的克隆声音
4. 输入以下文本 → 点击生成
```

**数字人文本（口播）：**
> "一个质检员，一天最多看两千个零件。到下午——眼睛已经花了。"

**备用文本（如果AI读得不够自然，换这个）：**
> "人工质检有极限。一天两千件，下午就开始漏。"

---

### 镜头 B1 — 工厂产线 B-roll (5s-9s)

**即梦文生视频 Prompt：**
```
A tired factory quality inspector working under warm yellow industrial lights, 
examining small electronic parts through a magnifying glass. 
Shot from side angle. Fatigue visible. 
Documentary style, natural lighting, 4K, cinematic.
```

**剪辑说明：** 数字人 D1 的最后 1-2 秒开始切入 B1，声音继续作为画外音。

---

### 镜头 B2 — 检测系统 B-roll (9s-13s)

**即梦文生视频 Prompt：**
```
Futuristic automated optical inspection machine scanning a green PCB circuit board. 
Blue laser scanning line moving across the board surface. 
Digital HUD overlay showing real-time data and green PASS indicator. 
Dark high-tech environment, dramatic blue lighting, professional industrial aesthetic.
```

---

### 镜头 B3 — 缺陷检出 B-roll (13s-17s)

**即梦文生视频 Prompt：**
```
Macro close-up of a circuit board with visible defect. 
Three red glowing circles appearing one by one to highlight: 
a cracked solder joint, a scratched trace, and an oxidized pad. 
Digital status changing from scanning to FAIL in red. 
Dark background, dramatic lighting, high contrast.
```

---

### 镜头 D2 — 数字人转场 (17s-22s)

**数字人文本：**
> "但我们的系统，从来不累。一秒一个，一秒一个——它不眨眼的。"

---

### 镜头 B4 — 效率对比 B-roll (22s-26s)

**即梦文生视频 Prompt：**
```
Split screen showing contrast. Left side: slow manual inspection, 
a hand with magnifier moving slowly. Right side: automated system 
processing parts rapidly with green PASS indicators flashing continuously. 
Timer comparison overlay: 3 minutes vs 2.8 seconds. 
Clean professional style with dramatic lighting difference between sides.
```

---

### 镜头 D3 — 数字人结尾 (26s-31s)

**数字人文本：**
> "今天一万两千件里面，有四十七个缺陷——人工根本没发现。想知道你们产线的漏检率？评论区告诉我。"

---

### 镜头 F1 — 结尾封面 (31s-35s)

**即梦文生图 Prompt（先文生图，再在剪映里加文字）：**
```
Dark professional background with subtle technology grid pattern. 
Clean minimalist design with ample negative space in center for text overlay. 
Blue and cyan accent lights at edges. 
Industrial technology aesthetic, no people, no text.
```

**剪映里添加的文字：**
- 大字：「你的产线，漏检率是多少？」
- 小字：「评论区聊聊」
- 底部：「OasysCoreCV」
